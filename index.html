AI 4 Change

AI 4 Change is a platform for students who want to practice AI through small projects and workshops, including seminars with AI professionals and enthusiasts, podcasts and blogs regarding AI, hackathons, AI projects that members can participate in for practical experience, and tutoring regarding topics related to AI.

Check out our programs

What We Do



Projects

Work with other students on AI projects that would last about a quarter and share your project/product with everyone




Podcasts

Be a part of our podcasts series on various AI topics and share your experiences/advices with others




Hackathons

24 hour coding events where students will be developing an AI project of their own within a group




Research Programs

Be part of the research team to work with other students and professors on an AI research project




Blogs

Join our blog team and write blogs on AI topics/projects and get featured on our blog.




Tutoring

Sign up to get tutored on AI topics and attend the workshops and seminars or become a tutor and help other students
