FAQ

What is it?

Spring Program is a 2 day event with interactive workshops and networking sessions for students interested in learning about Artificial Intelligence. It will feature a broad range of topics such as Introductory and Advanced Python and applying AI in various fields. Not only will these workshops help students learn and gain practical experience, but they will also allow them to network with other students attending the event.

Who is involved

AI 4 Change has brough in mentors from all around the world (California, Arizona, New York, UK, Turkey, India) who will be sharing their knowledge and experience to help students get started in AI.

Can I join?

Absolutely! There are no required skills and beginners are welcome!
